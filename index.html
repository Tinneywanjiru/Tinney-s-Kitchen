About Tinney's Kitchen

Meet Chef Whitney - Of Tinney's Kitchen

Cooking has always been more than just meals for me - it's a passion, an art, and a way to bring people together. My journey as a private chef started with a deep love for food and the joy of creating unforgettable dining experiences. From experimenting in the kitchen as a child to perfecting my craft professionally, every dish I create carries a story.

Through Tinney's Kitchen I bring homemade, high-quality meals to the table crafted with love and expertise. My goal is simple: to provide my clients with exceptional flavors, warmth, and a dining experience that feels both luxurious and comforting.

My Cooking Philosophy

At Tinney's Kitchen, I believe that great food starts with great ingredients. That's why I focus on using fresh, locally sourced produce and high-quality ingredients to create dishes that are not only delicious but also nourishing.

My cooking style is inspired by fusion cuisine blending African, French, and modern techniques, blending traditional flavors with a modern touch. Whether you're looking for an intimate private dining experience, event catering, or a custom meal plan, every dish is prepared with care, creativity, and attention to detail.
